## JSON Sentences

English:

    She woke him up with
    her Ramones ringtone "I Want
    to be Sedated"

JSON: (The new lines are replaced with "\n".)

    She woke him up with\nher Ramones ringtone \"I Want\nto be Sedated\"


## JSON Objects

Object:

    { person: true, name: "Roberta" }

    "{ \"person\": true, \"name\": \"Roberta\" }"
